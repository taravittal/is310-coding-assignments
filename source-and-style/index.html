About This Object

Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass, an American Slave is an autobiographical work published in 1845. In this book, Frederick Douglass recounts his experiences in slavery and his journey toward freedom. The text became one of the most influential works in American abolitionist literature and played a significant role in shaping public opinion about slavery.

This digitized version is preserved and made accessible through the HathiTrust Digital Library, which provides access to millions of scanned historical texts from research institutions around the world.
